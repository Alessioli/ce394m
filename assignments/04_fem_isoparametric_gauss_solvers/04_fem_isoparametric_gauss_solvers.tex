\section*{Datasheet}
The shape functions for a 6-noded triangle element are:
\begin{align*}
N_1 & = 2(1-\xi -\eta)^2 - (1 -\xi -\eta)\\
N_2 & = 2\xi^2 -\xi\\
N_3 & = 2\eta^2 -\eta\\
N_4 & = 4\xi(1 - \xi - \eta)\\
N_5 & = 4\xi\eta \\
N_6 & = 4 \eta(1 - \xi - \eta)\\
\end{align*}
